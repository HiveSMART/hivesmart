How we're helping

Supporting Kerr County & Central Texas Businesses in the Wake of the Floods.

The devastating floods in Kerr County and nearby communities have left businesses and nonprofits facing unimaginable loss—washed-out storefronts, damaged offices, and disrupted operations.But beyond the physical damage, we know this is about more than just rebuilding. It’s about the emotional toll—the stress, grief, and uncertainty that come after disaster strikes.

At HiveSMART, we are offering pro bono recovery services to businesses and nonprofits impacted by the floods. This includes:

• Emergency business recovery and action planning

• Help navigating relief funding and grants

• Sound healing and holistic stress relief sessions for business owners and staff

• Long-term strategies to rebuild with resilience

If your organization has been affected, please reach out. You don’t have to navigate this alone.
